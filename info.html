ℹ Project Info

🌱 Smart Irrigation System

This project helps monitor soil moisture, weather, crop type, and irrigation decisions from one simple dashboard.

  • Dashboard shows live-style sensor values and AI recommendations.
  • Actions page controls auto mode, pump start/stop, and irrigation history.
  • Analytics page shows moisture, water usage, and AI decision charts.
  • Settings page stores crop, threshold, location, and irrigation mode.

🎯 Project Aim

To reduce water wastage by suggesting irrigation only when soil moisture is low and rain probability is low.
